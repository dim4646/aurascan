What Is an Aura & Energy reading?

An Aura & Energy reading is a non-invasive, visual energy assessment that captures the energetic state of the body at a specific moment in time.

The reading provides a visual representation of:

  • Energy balance
  • Colour fields
  • Energy flow
  • Energy centres (chakras)

This experience is designed for self-awareness, reflection, and personal insight.

What You Can See During a Session

During your session, you may observe:
