What Is an Aura & Energy Scan?

An Aura & Energy Scan is a non-invasive, visual energy assessment that captures the energetic state of the body at a specific moment in time.

The scan provides a visual representation of:

  • Energy balance
  • Colour fields
  • Energy flow
  • Energy centres (chakras)

This experience is designed for self-awareness, reflection, and personal insight.

What You Can See During a Session

During your session, you may observe:

  • Aura colour visualization
  • Energy field distribution
  • Chakra activity overview
  • Overall energetic balance of the moment

Each session includes a guided explanation of the visuals, helping you interpret the energy patterns shown.
